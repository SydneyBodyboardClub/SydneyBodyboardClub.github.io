Sydney Bodyboard Club Invitational


Sydney Bodyboard Club Invitational is a competition for bodyboarding clubs around NSW. The idea is that clubs close to Sydney can come for a one day event, hang out with other bodyboarders, have some fun and on top of that raise some money for your club activities in 2018. The competition will be held every year and is a non profit event. All judges, organisations and participants are there to help the sport, and ultimately yourself. The prize will be the total amount raised in entry money, basically the winning club would take home around AU$1500 in cash, that can be used for the club activities and so on. BodyBoard King is donating a $150 voucher per division. NMD / Ben Player will be running a very special clinic for all participants. Minority will be providing some cool gear to the winners. VBC (Vintage Bodyboard Collectors) and Basic Boards will be giving a brand new designed board for the winner of the vintage category. Please promote this at your club through social media and word of mouth. Lets unify the sport and work together to be the best in what we do! And above all lets have some fun in the water.


Details:

  • When : 15 October 2017
  • Where : Whale Beach, Sydney
  • Clubs Involved : All NSW Clubs
  • Entry : AU$50
  • Judging : By athletes that are BIO Official judges
  • Deadline to enter : 6/10/2017
  • Structure : Sydney Bodyboard Club
  • Who can enter : Every club will nominate 1 athlete per category, athelets may only compete in one category, exception for VINTAGE; any club can enter as many vintage athletes they wish to.
  • Vintage Note : If you want to join the Vintage Competition and you don't belong to ANY club, you can join the Sydney Club or any other club just for the day.
  • Categories : Junior Men (under 18), Open Men (no age limit), Open Women (no age limit), Open Drop Knee (Male and Female – no age limit), Masters Men (over 35), Vintage (Male and Female – no age limit)
  • Note : In case a club doesn’t have an athlete for all 6 divisions, that club can “borrow” an athlete from any other club
  • Sponsored by : Sponsored by BodyBoard King, NMD, Minority Bodyboarding, Basic Boards & Vintage Bodyboarders Collectors
  • Main Prize : All entry money raised will go to the winning club
  • Bodyboard King : Donating a $150 voucher per division
  • NMD / Ben Player : Will be running a very special clinic for all participants
  • Minority : Will be providing some cool gear to the winners
  • VBC and Basic boards : Will be giving a brand new designed board for the winner of the Vintage category
