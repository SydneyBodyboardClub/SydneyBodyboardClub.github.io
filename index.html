SYDNEY BODYBOARD CLUB


Presentation Information

Our Preso Night is close! 24/11 6:30pm @ South Curl Curl Surf Club! All club members are invited and also all members from other clubs and friends! This year if you sign up for next year by 24/11 it’s AU$100 and if you bring a friend that has never been part of the club both of you will pay half that being $50! If you sign up after 24/11 it will be AU$150. All the signing ups for next year will be done via the website. Ben Player will present his movie FAR NORTH and then after a few beers and pizza the winners of 2017 will be announced sharing AU$2,000 in cash plus sponsorships and boards from NMD board co with more special prizes from Bodyboard King! An amazing prize from Bali Bodyboarding will also be presented!!!! Who will travel to Bali?


Mystics Trip Information

Mystics trip this year will be happening on the 2nd of December! It will be a day trip this time as it was all booked out. Let’s get together and have some fun! Last year was a blast and this year will be too. All clubs are invited, spread the word!


Last Comp

Today was the last comp of 2017, held in Curl Curl. Conditions proved tough but cleaned up as the day went on producing some fantastic waves for those who could find them. Josh being one of them, he managed to find a gem right on the buzzer and with that took a very nice score...
