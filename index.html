SYDNEY BODYBOARD CLUB


SBC 2017 Presentation

24/11/2017

On the 24th of November Sydney Bodyboard Club held their annual presentation to look back on what has been an amazing year of waves, mateship and Competitions. We started off the night with music and good vibes, followed by an amazing bodyboard movie with an assortment of delicious pizza’s. Next we watched a little clip by Reuben Riegler that looked back on the year bringing back memories from the whole, year giving everyone a little kick out of it. This year we had an amazing prize pool thanks to the club’s hard work doing the Bunning’s fundraisers, alongside that one member of the club had the opportunity to with a trip to Bali courtesy of Bali Bodyboarding. Then it was time to announce the over all results from the year; the results can be seen below along with some photos from the event.
