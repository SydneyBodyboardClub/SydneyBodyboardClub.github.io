SYDNEY BODYBOARD CLUB


SBC Comp #2

3/06/2018

With the swell maxing out at the northern beaches Sydney Bodyboard Club ventured to Manly in search of makeable waves. Manly was the backup location from Whaley and with fun waves we got to show Manly our great sport. The competitors threw down as usual but with mother nature on our side we had a few gems. Big rolls and even bigger flips were on the menu at South Stein, Joshua Kleve flipped his way into first place in the AAA, while, in AA Jack Wren rolled into first. As usual we couldn’t make this possible if it wasn't for our amazing team that works hard behind the scenes to make these comps possible. Big thanks also to the amazing sponsors that continue to help out our club; Bodyboard King, NMD Board Co, Bali Bodyboarding and Movement Magazine. See below for the video and some photos, keep posted on the socials for the next comp.
