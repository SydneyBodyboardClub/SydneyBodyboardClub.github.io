About

Sydney Bodyboard Club, Founded in 2002, is powered by volunteers passionate about the sport and having a good time, we are based on the Northern Beaches and host a bodyboard competitions on the first Sunday of each month (see the comp dates below). The club has been a stepping stone for some of the sport's greats including Ben Player and Dave Winchester. Join our friendly team to learn more about the sport, refine your competition skills and above all have fun with like-minded people. Every year there are great prizes to be won, which means that you need to turn up to every event and climb the ladder to hopefully guarantee yourself a spot in the top 3.



Competition Dates
